Chicken Pesto

Description

One of my absolute favourites. Chicken pesto and myself go way back, I've made this dish for more girls than I can count. You'll love it, she'll love it, and your ancestors will love you for it (impressing a woman with your cooking skills that is). You can make your own pesto if you really want to go all out.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 lb penne pasta
  • 1.5 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 oz cream cheese
  • 1/3 cup basil pesto
  • 1/4 cut grated parmesan
  • freshly cracked peper
  • 1 pinch crushed red pepper
  • 3 cup fresh spinach
  • 1/4 cup sliced sun dried tomatoes

Directions

  1. Cut chicken into 1 in pieces, add butter in a deep skillet and cook until slightly browned
  2. While chicken cooks, mince garlic, add to skillet for another minute
  3. Add uncooked pasta and broth to skillet, stir to disolve browned bits, put in a lid with medium high heat and bring broth to a boil
  4. Once at a full boil, stir pasta, replace lid, turn to medium-low. Allow to simmer for 8 minutes while stirring occasionally
  5. Once pasta is tender and broth is absorbed, add milk, cream cheese, pesto. Stir until fully mixed, then add parmesan
  6. Add fresh spinach and sun dried tomatoes, stirring until the spinach has wilted. Top with pepper and red pepper and you're set
