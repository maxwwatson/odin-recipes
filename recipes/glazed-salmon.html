Glazed Salmon

Glazed Salmon

Description

Sometimes, your date doesnt like pasta. She doesn't want fried chicken. So what is a man to do? Make a glazed salmon of course! It's incredibly easy, and tastes like candy.

Ingredients

  • Salmon: 4 6oz salmon fillets
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp blackening seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • Sauce: 3 tbsp butter
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 6 cloves garlic minced
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 3 tbsp water
  • 3 tbsp sriracha
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice

Directions

  1. Pat salmon dry, then season with salt, pepper, paprika, blackening seasoning
  2. Adjust rack to middle, preheat broiler
  3. Add butter and oil to oven safe skillet on med-high. Melt butter, add garlic, water, soy sauce, sriracha, honey, lemon juice, and cook for 30 sec until sauce is heated
  4. Add salmon skin side down, cook for 3. Continually baste with sauce.
  5. Broil salmon for 5-6 minutes, basting with sauce once during the broil until salmon is caramelized or cooked to desired doneness
  6. Garnish with minced parsley if desired
